About The Camp

Our monthly Training Camps are designed to give players a professional, challenging and enjoyable environment where they can develop their individual game and compete alongside other motivated players from across the region.

The camp is built around high repetition, ball contact, technical confidence and intelligent decision-making. Players will be challenged through progressive practices that develop individual detail before transferring into opposed and game-realistic situations.

01

Technical Detail

Ball mastery, receiving skills, passing detail, dribbling, finishing and 1v1 confidence.

02

Game Intelligence

Scanning, movement, decision-making, support angles and understanding when to play forward.

03

Physical Output

Speed, agility, changes of direction, balance, intensity and football-specific movement.

04

Competitive Practice

Small-sided games and realistic scenarios that allow players to express themselves.

Opportunities Beyond The Camp

These camps are an important part of the South West England Representative pathway. Players who attend throughout the season may be considered for further representative programme activity across the 2026/27 season.

Regular Academy Fixtures
Showcase Events
International Tournaments

The programme is designed to complement a player's current club football, not replace it. It gives players an additional environment to develop, test themselves and represent the South West.

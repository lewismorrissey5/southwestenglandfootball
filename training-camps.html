Monthly Development Programme

Training Camps

Three-hour monthly training camps for high-level U8-U13 players across the South West.

What it includes

Technical, tactical and physical development.

Our training camps are delivered once per month and are built around the demands of the modern game. Players work across technical detail, tactical understanding, physical movement and competitive game situations.

Sessions are delivered by UEFA licensed coaches and are designed for strong grassroots and JPL level players who are ready for a more challenging development environment.

01

3-hour camps

Longer training blocks allow players to experience detail, repetition, challenge and realistic game situations.

02

U8-U13 age groups

Age-appropriate practices and competitive environments for ambitious young players.

03

Selection pathway

Training camps act as a pathway into South West representative squads, academy fixtures and tournaments.

Development without interfering with your club.

We do not replace or interfere with your current club. We provide an additional opportunity for players to train with other high-level players, experience academy-style standards and develop through extra exposure.
